Let InEscrow work for you!

InEscrow offers solutions for both buying and selling agents.

For Listing Agents

1.


Sign up or login at www.inescrowapp.com, go to Listing agent tab

2.


Setup your listing and seller information

3.


Seller signs up to complete their deposit information

For Buying Agents

Step 1.


Sign up or login at www.inescrowapp.com, and go to buyers agent tab

Step 2.


Search for property address, and choose your home. Listing agent will receive a message to confirm your newly executed contract

Step 3.


Send link to your buyers to complete their portion of the transaction and send Earnest Money and option fee for contract
