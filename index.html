Founded in July of 2017 and based in Houston, Texas,

Werrus' primary objective is to develop and invest in the most profitable oil and gas projects in North America.

Focusing on both the upstream and service sector, Werrus stands out for its in-house geologic and industry expertise as well as flexible investment strategies.

We are proud that, along with the principals, each of the Werrus team brings a level of knowledge and experience that is invaluable to the longevity and growth of our investments.
